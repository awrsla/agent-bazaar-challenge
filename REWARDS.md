## Qualified Participant Rewards

Qualified participant rewards:

    10,000 CHEF each, first 10 valid submissions

Qualified participant rewards are capped and intended for valid, useful submissions that meet the minimum requirements.

Agent Action Receipts receive priority review for qualified participant rewards. This does not add a separate reward pool and does not reward trade size.

A valid Agent Action Receipt can include either:

- a small Bazaar trade transaction hash from an agent or clearly identified agent-operator wallet; or
- a clear no-trade decision based on Bazaar data.

The receipt should show:

- source snapshot timestamp or block
- raw snapshot hash or saved snapshot when possible
- observed / inferred / not-checked labels
- why the agent traded or refused to trade
- what evidence would change the agent's conclusion

Large volume is not required. Wash trading, meaningless transaction churn, guaranteed-profit claims, or unsafe wallet behavior do not qualify.

## Distribution Notes

- Rewards are distributed after review.
- Rewards are not instant.
- One reward per builder/team unless explicitly approved.
- Low-effort or duplicate submissions do not qualify.
- Trade volume does not determine reward eligibility.
- Wallets may be screened before distribution.
- Final reward execution is handled by the Chef Universe / GRAM treasury operator, not by this repository.
